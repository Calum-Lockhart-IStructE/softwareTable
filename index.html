IStructE - Software Selection Tool

This tool is designed to be used in accordance with the Institution of Structural Engineers' guidance on Quality Assurance and Quality Checking.

For codified design checks, it is strongly recommended that:

  • The software has been certified by a qualified engineer.
  • Full design checks are completed, rather than relying on partial checks.

As a best practice, any software used should be ISO 9001 compliant.

ISO 9001 is an international standard for quality management systems (QMS) that helps organizations ensure they consistently meet customer and regulatory requirements. It is based on principles like customer focus, leadership, process approach, and continuous improvement. Certification demonstrates a commitment to quality, efficiency, and risk management across various industries.
